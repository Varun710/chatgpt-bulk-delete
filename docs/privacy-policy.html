Privacy Policy – ChatGPT Multi-Delete

Last updated: November 28, 2025

Overview

ChatGPT Multi-Delete is a browser extension that allows users to select and delete multiple ChatGPT conversations at once directly within the ChatGPT interface.

Data collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, ChatGPT conversation content, browsing history, or other user information.

  • No account or identity information is collected.
  • No ChatGPT conversation text is collected or sent anywhere.
  • No analytics, tracking, or profiling is implemented.

How the extension works

All functionality runs locally in your browser. The extension modifies the ChatGPT page’s DOM to add selection checkboxes and a delete toolbar, and then triggers deletion actions that you explicitly initiate.

The extension only interacts with pages on chat.openai.com and chatgpt.com (as permitted by the host permissions you grant in your browser).

Permissions

The extension requests the minimum permissions necessary for its single purpose: allowing you to select and delete multiple ChatGPT conversations at once.

  • Host permissions for chat.openai.com and chatgpt.com are used only so the extension can inject its UI elements (checkboxes, toolbar) and perform user-initiated deletions within the ChatGPT interface.
  • activeTab (if present) is used only to interact with the currently active ChatGPT tab when you explicitly use the extension. No tab URLs or browsing history are stored or transmitted.

Third-party sharing

The extension does not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.
